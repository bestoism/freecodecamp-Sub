Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian pasta dish from Naples. It is made with just a few simple ingredients: spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes. This dish is known for its simplicity and bold flavors, making it a favorite among pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 dried red chili peppers (or to taste)
  • 4-5 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)
  • Chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Boil water in a large pot with a pinch of salt. Once boiling, add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Drain the pasta, saving a little bit of the cooking water.
  2. Prepare the Aglio e Olio Oil: Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped garlic and dried chili peppers. Saut√© for about 1-2 minutes, until the garlic turns golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Combine with Pasta: Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with the garlic oil. Stir well, adding a little bit of the reserved pasta water to make the sauce smoother and coat the pasta.
  4. Season: Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir everything together until the pasta is well coated with the garlic oil.
  5. Serve: Remove from heat, and optionally, top with grated parmesan cheese and chopped parsley. Serve hot.
